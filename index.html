Triangle Tracker

Triangle tracker will build an application that will help us know whether a triangle is isosceles, equilateral, scalene or NOT a triangle at all.

Different types of triangle

Equilateral triangles=sides must be equal.

    Isosceles triangles=two sides are exactly equal.

      Scalene triangles=none of the sides is equal.
